2. What SquareBrowser Is

SquareBrowser is a mobile web browser for Android designed to support intentional, accountable internet use. It includes:

  • Daily browsing time limits that pause the browser when exceeded
  • Strict Mode, which locks the browser completely until midnight with no override
  • A permanent, local browsing history that cannot be deleted from within the app
  • Per-domain usage statistics stored locally on your device
  • A built-in ad blocker

You understand and accept these features as core to how the app works.

3. The Permanent History Feature

SquareBrowser does not provide a clear history function. Every URL you visit is recorded in local storage on your device and cannot be deleted through the app.

This is intentional and is a key feature, not a bug. The permanent history exists to support honest self-accountability. It is stored only on your device, visible only to you, and is never transmitted to us or anyone else. You accept this design when you use the app.

4. No Private / Incognito Mode
