How It Works

Three steps to
intentional browsing

No willpower required. SquareBrowser makes good habits automatic.

01
Set Your Limit

Choose how much is enough

Pick your daily limit: 30 minutes, 1 hour, 2 hours, up to 6. Enable Strict Mode if you want no override possible. The timer shows in your address bar the whole time — a quiet reminder that your time is finite.

02
The Browser Stops You

Daily limit reached — time's up

When you've used your daily limit, the browser pauses and shows you a reminder. You can extend by 10 minutes once if needed — that small friction is often enough to make you close the app and do something real.

03
Strict Mode

Locked until midnight. No exceptions.

Enable Strict Mode and there is no override. The browser locks completely — access resumes at midnight. It's harsh by design. Because sometimes the commitment you make in the morning is the only thing that protects you at night.

Key Feature

Daily Browsing Limits

Set a hard daily cap on browsing. When time's up, the browser pauses — no endless scrolling. Choose from preset limits or fine-tune in 15-minute increments.

Strict Mode

No override. No "just 5 more minutes." Browser locks until midnight. For people who need hard commitments, not soft suggestions.

100% Private

Zero external servers. No analytics. No tracking of any kind. All data lives on your device and goes nowhere — because it literally has nowhere to go.

Permanent History

Your history cannot be deleted. That's accountability by design — knowing it's permanent changes how you browse.

Usage Stats

Daily time per domain. See exactly which sites are stealing your hours — local, private, honest data only you can see.

Ad Blocker Built In

Cleaner pages, faster loads, fewer distractions — right out of the box, no extensions or setup needed.

The honest answers

No — and that is the entire point. The permanent, undeletable history is what makes SquareBrowser an accountability tool. Knowing that every site you visit is recorded, and you can't erase it, changes how you browse. Your history lives only on your device and is never shared with anyone.
The browser locks completely and shows a countdown to midnight. There is no override, no "just 10 more minutes" option, and no workaround within the app. Access resumes automatically at midnight. This is deliberate — it's for people who want a hard stop they can't argue with in a weak moment.
Only you. SquareBrowser has no servers, no cloud sync, no analytics SDK, and makes no external network requests on your behalf. Everything — history, bookmarks, usage stats, settings — is stored in your device's private local storage. Nobody else can access it, including us.
