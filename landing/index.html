/ Privacy Pledge

Your data stays
with you.

We don't see
a single byte
of what you browse.

SquareBrowser has no servers to send data to. No analytics SDK is bundled. No crash reporting pings a remote endpoint. Your browsing history, bookmarks, and usage data are stored on-device and go nowhere — because there's nowhere to send them.
